About Us

Zyrtx Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a premier IT solutions provider based in Hetauda, Nepal. Our company is dedicated to transforming innovative ideas into impactful technological solutions. With a team of passionate and skilled professionals, we have been at the forefront of providing cutting-edge services to clients across various industries. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build long-lasting relationships with our clients, both locally and internationally.


Since our inception, Zyrtx Technologies has served over 50 clients, successfully completing more than 100 projects. Our expertise spans across website development, mobile application development, digital transformation, cloud services, and technology consulting. We pride ourselves on delivering customized solutions that cater to the unique needs of each client, ensuring that they achieve their business goals efficiently and effectively. Our team's dedication to innovation and continuous improvement drives us to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape.


At Zyrtx Technologies, we believe that our success is deeply rooted in the success of our clients. We are committed to providing top-notch IT services that empower businesses to thrive in the digital age. Whether it's developing a new website, creating a mobile app, or implementing a complex software solution, our team is equipped with the knowledge and experience to turn your vision into reality. Join us on our journey as we continue to make a positive impact through technology, one project at a time.
